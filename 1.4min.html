Guide

How to use the editor!

So when you came to this webapp, you wre greeted 4 different sections. Three of which are for coding.
To the very left, you have your HTML field,to the left-middle, you have the css,to the right-middle, you have js, and to the very right, you have an iframe view and a console window!
In order to start the program, you can just start typing (or press run from the edit menu at the top.
But now that we have a program, how are we supposed to save it?

Savng

FrostPlayground gives 3 different methods of saving.
If you want to save to your computer, use "Ctrl+e" or "Download' from the file menu.
If you want a data url for your application, click download url (only use for small applications)
If you're still testing and want to iteratively save it, use "Ctrl+s". Keep in mind that your browser is in charge of this data and it may be wiped randomly by your browser due to being temporary. Which brings us to our next topic!

Managing saves

So, you saved some program and want to get it back. So how are we supposed to do that?
In order to access the save management UI, you can press "Ctrl+o" or the "Load Save" item on the file menu. There you can rename the different files, delete saves, and merge them!
"But still, I don't want my saves to be wiped by my browser!" Well you're in luck because I created a feature based exactly on that! You can export (and import) your file database through the file menu! But why are all the files called "Untitled" and how do I change it?

Preferences

Say, you hate that there's NO WRAPPING!
Or want to turn off the autoindent!
Well, it's time to consult the preferences!These have settings to allow autoindent control, the indent when you press tab and shift+tab, wrap, and the Save Name!But what are all these controls for the text?

Text Hotkeys

In order to indent the current line, press tab (and un-indenting is shift+tab). There's also a hotkey for finding certain text and replacing it by pressing "Ctrl+h". When using quotes or parentheses, they will be automatically closed. When using a { it will also automatically indent.
To undo or redo, use "Ctrl+Z" to undo and "Ctrl+Shift+Z" or "Ctrl+Y" to redo! Keep in mind that if you modify while undoing, your redo history will be wiped.

Top Buttons

You may have noticed the 6 giant buttons with different names at the top.
The first four toggle the visibility of the HTML, CSS, JS, and the console window. Just click to make them disappear/reappear.
After that, there's a theme button (if you want light mode...)
And finally, we have the Fullscreen button which puts the iframe into Fullscreen!
